# Study Shelf Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension name
Study Shelf

## Short description
Turn open tabs into a calm, organized learning list.

## Detailed description
Study Shelf is a learning-focused new tab extension for people who keep too many useful pages open because they are afraid of losing them.

It groups your current tabs by website, lets you save important pages into a lightweight study list, and gives each saved page a simple place for status, tags, and one key takeaway. After saving a page, you can close the original tab with less pressure and come back to it later from your learning library.

What it helps with:
- Group open tabs by website
- Save individual pages or whole website groups
- Close tabs after saving them
- Reopen saved pages from the learning list
- Add learning status: To Learn, Learning, Learned
- Add tags and a short key takeaway
- Search and filter saved study pages
- Resize the two-panel layout for organizing or studying
- Switch between English and Chinese

Privacy note:
Study Shelf stores your saved pages and notes locally in Chrome storage. It does not sell user data, does not show ads, and does not send your saved study list to a server.

## Chinese description
Study Shelf 是一个学习型新标签页插件，适合经常打开很多资料网页、又舍不得关闭的人。

它会按照网站把当前打开的标签页分组，让你可以把重要页面保存到学习清单里，并为每个页面记录状态、标签和一句关键收获。保存之后，你就可以更放心地关闭原网页，下次再从学习清单里打开继续学习。

核心功能：
- 按网站聚合当前打开的网页
- 单独保存网页或保存整个网站分组
- 保存后关闭原标签页
- 从学习清单重新打开网页
- 设置学习状态：待学习、学习中、已学习
- 添加标签和关键收获
- 搜索和筛选已保存页面
- 左右两栏宽度可拖拽调整
- 支持中文和英文切换

隐私说明：
Study Shelf 会把你保存的页面和笔记存储在 Chrome 本地存储中。插件不出售用户数据，不展示广告，也不会把你的学习清单上传到服务器。
